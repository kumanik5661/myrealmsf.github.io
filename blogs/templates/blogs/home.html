About us

The brainchild of two ambitious teenagers, strengthened by relentless efforts of a group of young passionate minds, my_realms has just begun its journey of enlightening the whole world with its amazing talent. But the path is not smooth. Hurdles came, and my_realms found greater strength each time to overcome the hindrances. Now, we have found the wings to soar higher and higher. Come join us on this extraordinary flight of glory.

Words Expressed

Quoted by Walt Whitman — "Oh me! Oh life! of the questions of these recurring, Of the endless trains of the faithless, of cities fill’d with the foolish, —What good amid these, O me, O life? Answer - That you are here—that life exists and identity, That the powerful play goes on, and you may contribute a verse." And we're searching for all these verses that might otherwise remain unnoticed. We aim towards creating a haven which transcends the pain of this living world, encouraging the brave, to taste poetry, to cherish each word like a sip old wine.
